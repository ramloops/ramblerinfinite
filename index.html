about

photographer • building a portfolio • taking it seriously

I'm a photographer focused on landscapes, wildlife, and astrophotography. The work speaks for itself - I'm putting in the time to get technically solid shots and develop a consistent style.

Still building the business side, but I'm not just messing around with a camera. I travel for shots, wake up at 3am for astrophotography, and wait hours for the right wildlife moment. This is becoming more than a hobby.

I'm interested in collaborating with other photographers, aspiring models, or anyone working on creative projects. I prefer natural, unposed work and I'm more comfortable behind the camera than self-promoting, but the portfolio is growing steadily.

My following is small right now, but I'm focused on creating quality work rather than chasing social media metrics. Building something real takes time.
